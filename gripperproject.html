Gripper Project

Carnegie Mellon Univeristy, Engineering Design 1, 2018

As a part of the project I worked in a team of 5 to create a gripper attachment to a mechanical arm optimized for weight. This gripper was intended to hold waterbottle that weighs approximately 150g waterbottle with a relatively low coefficient of friction. The grip strength must also be enough to withstand circular dynamic motion. We were alloted a budget of $350 to complete this project.

Within this project team I was in charge of a large portion of the 3D modeling work in Solidworks. In addition to modeling, I also did a significant portion of the fabrication and assembly. As a team we performed simplified stress analysis and finite element analysis (FEA) using Solidworks. Our final gripper designed weighed 127g and successfully passed both nominal and dynamics testing.

Victoria Feng 2018
