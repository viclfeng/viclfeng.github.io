Crane Project

Carnegie Mellon Univeristy, Stress Analysis, 2018

As a part of the project I worked in a team of 3 to create a static crane that fit through an "obstacle course". This crane was intended to lift a one pound weigh 2in using a servo motor attached at the end. The crane was made from provided aluminum strips and screws, the goal of the project was to either optimize for weight or lift height. Our group chose to optimize for weight. We built the lightest crane weighing in at 5.6oz.

Within this project team I performed a lot of the hand analysis used to determine geometry. In addition to calculations, I also did a significant portion of the assembly.

Victoria Feng 2018
