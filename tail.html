Tail Attachment

Carnegie Mellon Univeristy, Robomechanics Lab, 2018

As a part of the CMU Robomechanics Lab I created this motor to gearbox attachment that is used to power a mechanical tail on the Ghost Minitaur robot.

This project served as an introduction to 3D modeling in Solidworks as well as my first exposure to engineering design.

If you would like to learn more about this project, please contact me at vlf@andrew.cmu.edu.

Victoria Feng 2018
